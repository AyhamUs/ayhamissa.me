INTERNSHIPS

Participating in short term positions and programs at Savone-Taylor provides a flexible and enriching experience, fostering diverse skill enhancement across various departments. These short-term engagements allow for quick adaptation, exploration of specific interests, and focused skill development. With networking opportunities, low entry barriers, and a fast learning curve, participants benefit from a versatile and inclusive environment. The condensed nature of these programs accelerates skill acquisition, enabling individuals to make meaningful contributions to projects, enhancing both personal and professional growth within the dynamic landscape of the cosmetics industry.

Product Development Intern

  • Work closely with our product development team to gain insights into the formulation of holistic cosmetics. Assist in researching and evaluating new ingredients, keeping abreast of industry trends. Contribute to the creation of prototypes and participate in testing phases.

Wellness and Sustainability Intern

  • Collaborate with our wellness and sustainability team to support initiatives related to holistic well-being and eco-friendly practices. Research sustainable sourcing options, assess environmental impact, and propose innovative solutions. Contribute to the development of educational materials on wellness and sustainability for internal and external audiences.

Customer Engagement/Marketing Intern

  • Work alongside our marketing team to develop customer-centric campaigns and promotional materials. Assist in social media management, engaging with our audience and promoting the company's values. Contribute to customer surveys and feedback analysis to enhance our products and services.

Retail Tech and E-Commerce Intern

  • Collaborate with our technology team to enhance our retail and online platforms. Gain experience in managing digital tools, optimizing user experience, and assisting in e-commerce strategies. Participate in the implementation of tech-driven solutions for customer engagement.
