ABOUT US

At Savone-Taylor, our journey is rooted in a deep love for cosmetics and a commitment to redefine beauty with a touch of sustainability. Founded by passionate individuals who believe in the self expression, confidence, and calming effects of cosmetics, we aim to create products that effortlessly enhance your beauty while being conscious of your skin health. Our commitment extends to sustainability, ensuring that our love for makeup aligns with our love for the planet.
