HUMAN RESOURCES

The Human Resources position at Savone-Taylor is a crucial role in cultivating a positive work environment. As a HR professional, you will oversee recruitment processes, implement employee wellness programs, and will also contribute to fostering a harmonious workplace culture. This role focuses on promoting diversity, inclusion, and a supportive atmosphere for personal and professional growth within the organization. Here you will have the opportunity to play a key role in shaping the overall employee experience within this company to ensure a stable and commutative space to work.

JOB REQUIREMENTS

  • HR Manager: Bachelor's degree in Human Resources or related field, 5+ years of HR experience, strong knowledge of employment laws. 
  • Employee Relations Specialist: HR-related degree, 4+ years of experience in employee relations, conflict resolution skills.
  • Compensation and Benefits Analyst: Bachelor's degree in HR, finance, or related field, 3+ years of experience in compensation and benefits analysis.
