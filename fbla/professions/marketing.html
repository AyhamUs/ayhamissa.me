MARKETING TEAM

As a part of the marketing team for Savone-Taylor, you'll be at the forefront of creativity, tasked with conceptualizing and implementing innovative marketing strategies. Your role extends beyond traditional campaigns to ideating unique approaches that capture the pure and holistic nature of our brand. From creating engaging visual content to exploring sustainable marketing initiatives, you'll be tasked to engage and capture the consumers with resonating promotions. This position offers an opportunity to become well versed with the company and encompass all facets of the brand to marketing solutions.

JOB REQUIREMENTS

  • Digital Marketing Specialist: Bachelor's degree in Digital Marketing or relevant field, proficiency in SEO and SEM, 3+ years of experience in digital marketing. 
  • Social Media Coordinator: Bachelor's degree in Communications or Marketing, social media management experience, creative skills in content creation. 
  • Product Marketing Manager: Bachelor's degree in Marketing or related field, 4+ years of product marketing experience in the cosmetics industry. 

JOB OPENINGSApply

  • Digital Marketing Specialist
    The Digital Marketing Specialist role at Savone Taylor Cosmetics involves crafting and implementing digital strategies to elevate brand presence and drive sales. Responsibilities include devising engaging social media content, managing paid advertising campaigns, and analyzing performance metrics to optimize marketing efforts. Collaborating with cross-functional teams, staying updated on industry trends, and conducting market research are key aspects of the role. The ideal candidate possesses a bachelor's degree in Marketing or a related field, digital marketing experience in the cosmetics sector, proficiency in relevant tools and platforms, strong communication skills, and a creative mindset. Benefits include competitive salary, health benefits, professional development opportunities, employee discounts, and a dynamic work environment.

  • Social Media Coordinator   
    The Social Media Coordinator role at Savone Taylor Cosmetics entails strategizing, curating, and executing captivating content across various social media platforms to bolster brand awareness, engagement, and community growth. Responsibilities include crafting visually appealing posts, engaging with followers, coordinating influencer partnerships, and analyzing performance metrics to optimize social media initiatives. The ideal candidate holds a bachelor's degree in Marketing or related field, possesses previous experience managing social media for brands, demonstrates exceptional communication skills, and stays abreast of social media trends and best practices. Benefits encompass competitive salary, health benefits, professional development opportunities, employee discounts, and a collaborative work environment within the dynamic cosmetics industry.

  • Product Marketing Manager       
    Savone-Taylor, an esteemed brand in personal care products, is seeking a proactive and results-oriented Product Marketing Manager to spearhead strategic initiatives driving brand growth and consumer engagement. This pivotal role involves developing comprehensive marketing strategies, conducting market research, and collaborating cross-functionally to ensure effective product positioning and messaging. The ideal candidate will possess a blend of strategic thinking, creative flair, and analytical acumen, with a proven track record in product marketing within consumer goods. They will excel in crafting compelling narratives, orchestrating integrated marketing campaigns, and leveraging data insights to optimize performance. This opportunity offers a dynamic environment where innovation is encouraged, and success is measured by the positive impact made on consumers' lives through exceptional products and meaningful brand experiences.

SALARY RANGES OF POSITION

  • Digital Marketing Specialist: $60,000 - $90,000 per year
  • Social Media Coordinator: $45,000 - $65,000 per year
  • Product Marketing Manager: $70,000 - $100,000 per year
